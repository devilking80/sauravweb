BGS Global Institute of Medical Sciences

BGS Global Institute of Medical Sciences or BGSGMIS was established in 2012 in Bangalore, Karnataka. It is one of the leading and the most popular privately established medical colleges in Karnataka. 

BGS Global Institute of Medical Sciences is affiliated to the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS) and has been established to inform quality medical education and generate dedicated healthcare professionals for services in the society.
BGS Global Institute of Medical Sciences Vision is to be recognized as a centre of excellence providing quality medical training and comprehensive health care in a socially responsible manner.

BGS Global Institute of Medical Sciences Admissions

Students who apply should have passed the P.U.C examination (two years) with Physics, Chemistry & Biology. The Selection of students is based on the Common Entrance Test (C.E.T) conducted by the Karnataka Government or COMED-K conducted by the Consortium of Pvt. Medical Colleges of Karnataka.

BGS Global Institute of Medical Sciences Facilities

  • Medical College- Following the MCI norms, all the departments of Anatomy, Physiology, Bio-Chemistry, Pathology, Pharmacology, Microbiology, Forensic Medicine, and Community Medicine have been designed.
  • Lecture Halls- The Institute has updated lecture theatres which are very spacious, accommodating over 250 students, and are facilitated with audio-visual equipment like projectors, etc. Digital learning has been incorporated by recording live lectures, e-learning, highly secured exam halls.
  • College Library- A huge library with over 400 students’ seating capacity, along with journals and books with a computer lab, internet, and audio-visual equipment.
  • Hostels: The institute has separate hostel facilities for boys and girls, both equipped with all necessities like hostel mess, security, and recreation rooms.
  • Health Club- Indoor and outdoor games, gymnasium, badminton court and table tennis equipment are available for students to exercise and have a healthy attitude.
  • Cafeteria- A separate cafeteria serving healthy and nutritious food at an affordable price is available for students. There is another canteen for patients and family in the institute as well.
  • Teaching Hospital- The institute has a well-equipped teaching hospital for students with the first year M.B.B.S. students having a total of 300 beds. The Medical & Allied i.e. General Medicine, Paediatrics, TB & Chest, Dermatology, and Psychiatry have 120 beds; the Surgical & Allied i.e. General Surgery, Orthopaedics, Ophthalmology, and ENT have 140 beds; and the Obstetrics & Gynaecology having 40 beds. There are 20 ICU beds available which include PICU, NICU, ICU, and ICCU.

BGS Global Institute of Medical Sciences Faculty

BGS Global Institute of Medical Sciences has a huge team leading the faculty. The teaching members of the staff exceed over 200 medical professionals acting as associate professors, assistant professors, tutors, and residents who are experts in their fields. All the professors and Head of Departments have earned the M.B.B.S. degree along with M.D. or M.S in their fields.

There are 13 clinical departments, 5 para-clinical departments, and 3 pre-clinical departments. Each department is head by an experienced doctor.

There are also 380 staff nurses and over 180 non-teaching staff helping out the professors and students whenever assistance is needed.

Hostel

  • BGS Global Institute of Medical Sciences offers separate hostels for both boys and girls with spacious, clean, and well-furnished living places and leisure rooms.
  • BGS GIMS has healthy and designed two bed room flats with all the fundamental and modern facilities are provided for Senior Residents and Junior Residents
  • A separate canteen, adjacent to the hospital, is also made available for patients and caretakers.
