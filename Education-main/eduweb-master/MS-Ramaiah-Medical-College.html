RMC Admission

Ramaiah Medical College offers admission to UG, PG, Super Specialty, Fellowship, and Doctoral Courses in the field of Medical and Health Sciences. Ramaiah Medical College (RMC) is a prestigious Private Medical College located in Bangalore, Karnataka.

  • Candidates will have to apply forNEET-UG
  • examination conducted by NTA to take admission in MBBS course
  • For BPT, candidates will be selected based on the merit in the qualifying examination
  • Candidates applying for MBBS and BPT will have to take the form online from the official website of RMC, Bangalore
  • Candidates applying for the postgraduate courses have to apply online through the official website of NEET-PG conducted by NBE
  • Candidates should have obtained MBBS degree from any college recognized by the Medical Council of India for MD/MS course
  • For MPT, students should have obtained BPT degree and are selected on the basis of merit
  • For DM/MCH, selection is done on the basis of rank obtained in NEET-SS
  • examination by the National Board of Examination, New Delhi
  • To apply for UG courses, candidates must have atleast passed 10+2 or equivalent exam from a recognized board with 50% marks in aggregate are eligible
  • Admission is done strictly on the basis of Merit in the entrance test
  • For PhD courses, candidates should have MD/MSc degree with a minimum of 55% to become eligible. For PhD in Physiotherapy, candidates should have passed MPT degree  

Candidates seeking admission to MBBS, M.D/M.S, and D.M/M.Ch courses at Ramaiah Medical College first need to clear NEET, NEET PG, and NEET SS respectively, and then appear for the NEET Counseling conducted by Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) or Medical Counseling Committee (MCC). For the rest of the courses, the college conducts the entire admission process on its own and the candidates need to apply on the official website of the college for the same.

Ramaiah Medical College offers the MBBS course for a duration of 5.5 Years including one year of Compulsory Rotating Internship. MBBS at RMC covers all preclinical, para-clinical, and clinical disciplines through theory classes and clinical training as mandated by the Medical Council of India (MCI). Admissions to the MBBS program at Ramaiah Medical College are done on the basis of the NEET-UG followed by Karnataka NEET Counseling conducted by the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA).
