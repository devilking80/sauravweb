Jain University

Jain University is a prestigious university located in the Silicon Valley of India, Bengaluru. Established in the year 1990 as the Sri Bhagwan Mahaveer Jain College, the university gained the status of a deemed-to-be-university in 2009. The university has a total of seven campuses and Jain University main campus is located in Bangalore. In the Jain University NIRF Ranking 2023, it bagged 68th place in the university category. In the engineering category, it was placed in 115th place, and CMS Business School secures 85th position in India.

  • Jain University offers various UG, PG, Research, and Vocational courses through its 11 Schools and 25 Departments. 
  • The university offers around 71 UG and 51 PG courses with around 18,000+ students. 
  • B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, and MCA are the most popular courses offered by Jain University. 
  • The university also offers various courses in several disciplines through its online programs and distance learning platform. 
  • Jain University Admission to all the UG and PG courses is based on Jain Entrance Test (JET) conducted by the university.

Jain University has been consistently ranked among one of the top private universities with a good placement record. The highest package offered during Jain University Placement 2023 stood at INR 30 LPA for a B.Tech student. The average package stood at INR 5.85 LPA for M.Tech and INR 6.92 LPA for B.Tech. Amazon, Deloitte, IBM, Accenture, TCS, Capgemini, Wipro, and Infosys are some of the top recruiters at Jain University Placements.

Jain University Admission

The minimum qualification required to apply for JAIN ENTRANCE TEST(JET) is a pass in the 10+2 examination in Science / Commerce / Arts from PUC / ISC / CBSE or equivalent board. A course recognized as equivalent thereto by the University.

Online Application:

A non-refundable application fee for the preferred program should be paid through Online Payment gateway Mode with the valid Credit Card / Debit Card or Net Banking facility.

Offline Application:

Candidates can purchase a non-refundable online application coupon from the Admissions Office and redeem the same by entering the specific coupon code in the Online Payment Gateway under the heading 'Coupon Code'.

Jain University Result

Jain University has recently released the results for all PG and UG degree programs under JAIN Online whose examinations were held in January 2023, and February 2023 respectively. The results for the December 2022 examination for all UG degree courses offered by Jain University School of Engineering and Technology are also announced. 

How to Check Jain University Result

Jain University announces the results for its various semester and annual examination on its official result portal. Candidates need to follow the steps mentioned below to check Jain University Result 2023:

  • Step 1: Visit the official result portal of Jain University. (results.jainuniversity.ac.in)
  • Step 2: Search for the ‘Notification’ section on the homepage.
  • Step 3: The result links for various courses will be available in the notification section. Select the desired result link.
  • Step 4: Enter the Registration Number and click on ‘Show Result’. 
  • Step 5: The result will be displayed in the form of a PDF. Download the result for future reference.

Jain University B.Tech Scholarship

FET JU believes in promoting higher education among deserving and meritorious students. It offers a number of scholarships under various categories. The scholarships are granted to students on the basis of scores secured in CET, COMEDK, Uni-GAUGE, and JEE Main & Advanced. For CBSE / ICSE board students, scholarships will be awarded on a merit basis. Scholarships are disbursed on a first-come, first-served basis, after the scrutiny of the scorecards/ mark sheets and the subsequent approval from the university officials. To be eligible for the Meritorious Scholarship applicants must fulfil all the requirements given below:

  • Must be a citizen of India and residing in the country
  • Students who have secured a minimum of 60% and above in the 12th/2nd PUC/equivalent examination board prescribed as per AICTE and UGC.
Scholarship Category Eligibility
JET Meritorious Scholarship Applicants who have secured exceptional marks in the JET examinations
Sports Scholarship Applicants who have won/participated at the National level in Super-a Games or B-Games in India.
Defence Scholarship This scholarship is granted to applicants whose parents are either serving in the Armed Forces or are Ex-Defence personnel.
Caste Scholarship (SC & ST) In accordance with Government regulations, scholarships will be offered to students belonging to SC/ST categories.

JEE (MAIN) Based Scholarship for B.Tech Applicants

Based on scores availed in JEE-MAIN, conducted by NTA, B.Tech aspirants applying to JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) - Faculty of Engineering and Technology shall be eligible for the grant of the meritorious scholarships

Eligibility: Students who have secured a minimum of 60% and above in the 12th/2nd PUC/equivalent examination board prescribed as per AICTE and UGC.
