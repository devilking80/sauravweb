Garden City University

Garden City University was established under the Karnataka State Act No. 47 of 2013 and is a part of the Garden City Group of Institutions. The university is approved by UGC and is ISO certified. The university is ranked 31 by India Today 2021 for Fashion Design. Garden City University Bangalore, through nine schools of study, offers UG, PG, and PhD courses to students.

The university offers various UG, PG, Diploma, and Ph.D. programs across arts, commerce, management, science, and medical streams from its 7 schools of study. Under UG, it offers BA, B.Sc, B.Com, BHM, BPT, BBA & BCA & under PG level it offers MA, M.Com, MBA, MCA, M.Sc, MTTM, and LL.M. Admissions to the university are based on entrance exams followed by personal interview.
