MSRIT

M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology was founded in 1962 by Late Dr M Ramaiah, a renowned visionary, philanthropist, and pioneer in developing several landmark infrastructure projects in India. Dr M.S. Ramaiah envisioned MSRIT as an institute of excellence providing quality and affordable education in response to the shortage of talented engineering professionals required to build a modern India. As per NIRF Ranking 2023, M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology was ranked 55 in the college category, 67 in the engineering category, 62 in the pharmacy category, 38 in the medical category, 14 in the dental category, and 17 in architecture.

MSRIT is regarded as one of India's finest names in engineering education, having produced approximately 35,000 engineering professionals who hold positions of responsibility worldwide. Ramaiah Institute of Technology courses includes B.E, B.Arch, MBA, MCA, M.Tech, and research programmes. M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology admissions are done on the basis of entrance exams like KEA/COMEDK for B.E, NATA for B.Arch, KMAT / CMAT / MAT/ PGCET for MBA, etc. It also grants access to management quota seats. 

The M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology placement rate is 95%, and more than 150 companies visit the college for recruitment every year. The MSRIT highest package offered to a student for the year 2022 was Rs 50 lakh per annum. MSRIT has a dedicated placement and training cell that assists students in improving their skills and preparing them for interviews at prospective workplaces. M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology facilities comprise of a hostel, a gym, an internet centre, a library, and many more.

M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology Selection Criteria

12th Marks and Graduation:

Candidates seeking admission into any bachelor’s programme at M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology Bengaluru should have a minimum of 50% aggregate in their 12th standard exams. The institute from which they clear their 12th standard should be a recognised one along with proper certificates. For admission at M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology for PG courses candidates need to have a graduate degree on relevant subject. 

Entrance Exams:

For MSRIT courses like B.Tech, B.Arch MBA, M.Tech and MCA candidates need to clear the respective entrance exams.

M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology B.E Eligibility

Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with Chemistry/ Biotechnology/ Biology/ Computer Science/ Electronics as optional subject and have obtained 45% marks (40% for SC/ST/Cat-1 and OBC candidates).

M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology B.E Selection Criteria

  • For granting admission in the B.E course, the institute considers the score of KCET/COMEDK exam.
  • The qualified candidates will get a call to attend the counselling round of the qualifying exam.
  • During the counselling, applicants need to register online, get their documents verified, fill the choice of course and college.
  • Lastly, based on the rank and choices, the concerned authority will allot seats to candidates and such candidates need to report at the allotted college to participate in further admission rounds.
