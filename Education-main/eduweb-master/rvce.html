RVCE:

RV College of Engineering (RVCE) is a self-financing Engineering College (AICTE approved) which was established in the year 1963 with only three branches of Engineering: Civil, Electrical & Mechanical. RCVE offers 12 specializations under UG Programmes in Engineering, 16 specializations under Master's Degree programmes and Doctoral studies. RCVE offers admission to over 1200 students per year. RCVE is known for their quality education, excellence in technical studies, innovative teaching methods and consulting & Research through an outcome-based curriculum.

RVCE has ranked 89th in the engineering category of NIRF Rankings 2022. RCVE offers MCA at the PG level which is considered a flagship programme and the duration of the course is of 3 years. Admission to the various courses of Engineering is provided on the basis of the merit scored by the candidates in the Entrance Exams. The Entrance exams accepted by RCVE are GATE, K PGCET/KMAT or its equivalent entrance exams. The application form for B.E, M.Tech and MCA (Management Quota) is currently available on the official website. Check RV College Of Engineering Admission

RVCE Scholorships

RVCE offers various scholarships to students according to eligibility, merit and financial assistance to students following the criteria set by the college. The following are some of the scholarships offered to the students at RCVE Bangalore:

  • As a financial Incentive INR 2,500 is offered in the second year of representation, INR 3000 is offered in 3rd year and INR 3500 is offered in 4th year.
  • Sports Reimbursements for students are provided by the Govt. of Karnataka
  • For minorities & girl children, financial assistance up to INR 60,000 is provided.
  • Meritorious students who qualified for admission through Entrance exams are provided with some concession in their total fees structure for the course
