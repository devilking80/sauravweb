Dr BR Ambedkar Medical College, Bangalore

Dr. BR Ambedkar Medical College & Hospital (BRAMC), Bangalore, was founded in July 1981 by Ananda Social and Educational Trust. The college is recognised by the Medical Council of India (MCI). The campus spreads over 25 acres of land. BRAMC Bangalore includes one undergraduate and 18 postgraduate programmes. The BRAMC Bangalore admissions will be based on the scores obtained in the NEET/NEET PG. The candidate must meet the BRAMC Bangalore eligibility criteria and cutoff before applying for the desired course. The college provides multiple facilities on campus. The BRAMC Bangalore facilities include separate accommodations, a library, sports, a mess, a gym, transportation, and many other facilities

The institute focuses on delivering high quality, comprehensive, integrated medical education including innovative thoughts towards modern technology and research. The vision of the institute is to provide a world-class integrated healthcare system, with a focus on compassionate patient care to the underprivileged people. The mission of the institute is to bring healthcare to International standards within the reach of every individual.

Hostel

  • Separate hostel with a qualified warden is arranged in the same campus for girls, with full security measures. Hostel is compulsory for all the students admitted to this college. Food is provided in the hostel mess.
  • Full time warden is appointed to take care of the students and to see discipline .Security personnel are also appointed round the clock.
  • The college runs hostels for the men and women students separately within the campus at Kadugondanahalli. The hostel buildings with spacious rooms, lounges, recreation room having colour television sets in each of the hostels, with all other modern amenities, are built to ensure comfortable stay of the inmates, and a proper atmosphere for study. The women students' hostel, with a view to ensure absolute safety of the inmates, is fenced properly and secured.
