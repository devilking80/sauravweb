MS Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences (MSRUAS)

MS Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences (RUAS), Bangalore is committed to work on the philosophy of inclusiveness and service to society and to train its faculty members and students to contribute significantly to the growth of Technology, Science and society through their imaginative, creative and innovative pursuits. University was created by Integrating all the Institutions running under the trust and all Institutions have been converted to the faculties of the University. 
The university provides various courses at different levels Undergraduate, postgraduate, PG Certifucate, PG Diploma and Doctoral programs  in many streams such as Engineering, Design, Science, Commerce, Business Administration, Dental Studies, and Pharmacy courses, etc.

Reva University Highlights

MS Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences (MSRUAS) also referred to as the Ramaiah University, is a private-funded university established in 2013 under the Karnataka University Act. The university has two campuses (Gnanagangothri and Peenya) across Bangalore. Ramaiah, through its 8 faculties, two schools of study, and two campuses offers UG, PG, and PhD courses across Engineering and Technology, Management and Commerce, Art and Design, Mathematical & Physical Sciences, and other streams. It is approved by the AICTE and UGC and QS I-Gauge has awarded Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences with a Gold University Rating in 2022. The university is ranked 62 by NIRF 2022 for Pharmacy. 

Course & Fees

Undergraduate

The university provides many undergraduate degrees such as B.Tech, B.Des., B.Sc.(Hons), B.Com.(Hons), BBA, BDS, B.Pharm., Pharm.D, and BHM degree. Admissions to 7 specializations to B.Tech courses are done on the basis of 10+2 pass certificate with Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry/ Biotechnology/ Biology or equivalent from a recognized board with at least 45% marks (40% in case of candidates belonging to reserved category) in the above subjects taken together. All the undergraduate admissions are done on the basis of 10+2 pass certificate in a relevant discipline along wth valid marks obtained in RUASAT. Entrance exams like NEET for BDS are taken for various admissions in different specializations.

Postgraduate

The university also provides numerous PG degrees through its departments like M.Tech., M. Des., MBA, M.Sc., M.Com, Masters in Public Health, MDS, MHA, and M.Pharm. Degree. Admissions to UG courses are done on a valid UG degree in a relevant stream done from a recognized university. For M.Tech admission, a candidate must possess a genuine B.Tech/B.E. degree from a recognized university in a related specialization and valid score in PGCET.

Ph.D. Degree

The university also invites applications from all eligible candidates for the research level Ph.D. programs among various specializations with a postgraduate degree in the relevant discipline with requisite aggregate marks. The university also grants admissions to various certificates and diploma courses after a postgraduate degree in some special courses.

MSRUAS Admission Process: 

MSRUAS admission to all UG/ PG courses will be granted on the basis of min eligibility in Class 12/ last qualifying exam, RUASAT Test and merit. 

MS Ramaiah University (MSRUAS) admissions are mainly based on entrance test scores. Students applying for the UG/ PG are required to appear for RUAS AT. PhD candidates must appear for RUAS- AT followed by a presentation round. Moreover, courses like BTech, BDS and MDS accept national-level entrance scores like JEE and NEET, respectively.

Scholorship

Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences provides two types of scholarships. Students can choose to apply for an Admission Test Scholarship or a Merit Scholarship. It is to note that Merit Scholarship can either be a Scholarship of Excellence or a Scholarship of Distinction. Besides, students can avail loans, government and non-government scholarships. In an academic year, Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences students are eligible for only one Admission Test or Merit Scholarship.

MSRUAS Scholarship Key Points

  • Students who have received Merit Scholarships in a respective year are also eligible for the scholarship the next year provided they meet the eligibility criteria, i.e. CGPA marks
  • Similarly, students who don’t qualify for Merit Scholarships in a respective year are also eligible for the scholarship the next year provided they meet the eligibility criteria, i.e. CGPA marks
