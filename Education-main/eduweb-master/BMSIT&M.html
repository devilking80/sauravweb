BMSIT

BMS Institute of Technology and Management was established in the year 2002 and is governed by BMS Educational Trust founded by Sri B M Sreenivasaiah and his illustrious son, Sri B S Narayan. It is a private, un-aided, non-minority, autonomous college which is situated in Bangalore, Karnataka. The college is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi. It has been recognised by UGC and has also been approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The institute has also been accredited by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) with an “A”  grade along with NBA. To generate capable engineers who can handle such obstacles, BMSIT has flawlessly integrated its high-performing academic, personality development, and administrative subsystems

The BMSIT Bangalore courses are offered to interested candidates at the undergraduate as well as postgraduate levels. There are a total of 5 courses offered to the candidates in the stream of engineering, computer applications and management. These courses are offered in the offline regular mode to the candidates. The BMS Institute of Technology and Management admission to all the courses is offered on the basis of the candidate's performance in the respective entrance exam as well as the last qualifying exam. KCET (for BE courses), GATE/Karnataka PGCET (for ME/M.Tech courses) and KMAT/Karnataka PGCET (for MBA & MCA courses) are some of the entrance exams that are accepted by the institute for admission.

Admission

The candidates shall have passed the 2nd year Pre University or 12th standard or equivalent examination held preceding the entrance test with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with one of the following subjects - Chemistry, Bio-technology, Computer Science, Biology or Electronics. The Candidate should have secured not less that 45% of aggregate marks in optional subjects with English as one of the languages for admissions to engineering.

BMS Admission Registration 

The application form can be obtained online through the university website.

  • To fill up the COMEDK application form, candidates must register themselves on the official website.
  • He/she must provide all the necessary personal details.
  • After previewing and ensuring that the details, the candidate will be able to successfully register. He/she will receive credentials (user id and password) for the application form.
  • After using the credentials, the candidate must log in and fill the other details remaining in the application form. At this stage, he/she must also select the course to be pursued.
  • He/she must upload a scanned recent photograph along with the scanned copies of the required documents.
  • The applicant must select three nearby examination centers from the given list.
  • The payment must be done online and it is non-refundable.
  • The application fee is INR 1200 for PCB/PCM students and INR 1500 for PCMB students.
  • After the payment of the application fee, a transaction ID will be displayed which must be noted down for later use.
  • After this is completed, it is compulsory to take a print out of the form for future reference
