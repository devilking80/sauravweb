The Oxford Medical College Hospital and Research Centeris a premier Institute. It is located in Bangalore, Karnataka. Institute offers 25 courses which are Degree in Full Time mode. Overall, Institute has diversified 4 UG courses. Institute enjoys a good reputation for courses like MS, MD, MBBS, B.Sc. in the streams of Medicine & Health Sciences Students can opt courses from these. Over the period, 

 The Oxford Medical College Hospital and Research Center has gained expertize through its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of Orthopaedics, Toxicology, General Medicine, Pathology, Anaesthesia, Ophthalmology, Obstetrics & Gynecology, General Surgery, Dermatology, Pediatrics, Microbiology, Physiology, Anatomy, Community Medicine, ENT, Biochemistry, Psychiatry, Anaesthesia Technology, Echo Cardiology & Cardiac Technology, Medical Laboratory Technology.
