Sapthagiri Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre

Sapthagiri Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre are established by Sri Srinivasa Educational & Charitable Trust. It is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences and approved by Medical Council of India. It offers graduate and postgraduate courses in medical science. The teaching hospital offers a range of clinical specialties, modern diagnostic facilities offering integrated care and has all the amenities of an ultra-modern hospital. The faculty of institute is highly qualified, dedicated and experience in the field of medical science education. Admission to MBBS at Sapthagiri Institute of Medical Sciences And Research Centre is based on NEET UG. 

Over the period, Sapthagiri Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre has gained expertize through its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of Oncology, Gastroenterology, Endocrinology, Radiology, Cardiology, Pharmacology, Anaesthesia, Biochemistry, Dermatology, General Medicine, Microbiology, Pediatrics, Pathology, Physiology, Psychiatry, Community Medicine, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Ophthalmology, ENT, General Surgery, Orthopaedics, Anaesthesia Technology, Echo Cardiology & Cardiac Technology, Perfusion Technology, Medical Laboratory Technology. Courses offered by institute are recognized by reputed approving body like NMC. Institute has a capacity of taking 504 students overall under all courses. Institute has a well supported campus with facilities like Auditorium, Boys Hostel, Cafeteria, Girls Hostel, Gym, Hospital / Medical Facilities, Hostel, Labs, Library, Sports Complex, Wi-Fi Campus.
