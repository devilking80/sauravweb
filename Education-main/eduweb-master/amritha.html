Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bangalore

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bangalore is ranked 8th Best University in India by NIRF, Govt of India and awarded ‘Institution of Eminence’ tag to Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University to emerge as World-Class Teaching and Research Institution by Government of India. Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University offers degree courses in the discipline of Engineering, Communication, Data Science, etc. Admissions are done on the basis of merit and the cutoff criteria of the institute.

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham has five campuses at different locations with more than 18,000 students studying in various disciplines.

  • Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Banglore(Karnataka)
  • Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Amritapuri (Kollam) 
  • Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Coimbatore 
  • Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Kochi Campus 
  • Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Mysore Campus

 scholarships

Amrita University Bangalore provides scholarships. All M.Tech programs at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham are approved by AICTE for GATE scholarships. All GATE qualified candidates will receive a Post Graduate Scholarship of INR 12,400 per month and Non-Gate qualified students can avail scholarships between INR 50,000 to INR 1 lakh per year

Hostels

HOSTELS

There are 6 hostels in the campus, two for girls and four for boys.

Students seeking admission to the hostel have to apply separately in the prescribed application form. Staying in the hostel is mandatory for all the students whose parents are not residing in places accessible to institution transport.

The hostels are under the control of the Chief Warden. There is a deputy Chief Warden, Faculty Wardens and Resident Wardens to assist the Chief Warden. Pure and wholesome vegetarian food is served in buffet style in the hostel. Mathura, Yashodha and Devaki Hostels are having dining facilities for the students

Each hostel block is supervised by a Resident warden and an Assistant Warden. They stay in their respective hostel block and take care of students and attend to their needs.
