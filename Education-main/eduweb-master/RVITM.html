RVITM

RV Institute of Technology and Management (RVITM), Bangalore Established in 1999 by Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust (RSST), R V Institute of Technology and Management (RVITM) is the engineering college located in Bangalore. RVITM Bangalore offers BE/ BTech in various specialisations such as Computer Science Engineering, Information Science Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. This institute has been recognised by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)

Admission

The candidates can apply for admission through online or offline mode.

The candidates can apply online through the official website of the institute or can obtain the application form from the admission office of RVITM. The admission will be done on the basis of KEA CET quota and Management quota. The selection is based on marks obtained in KEACET AND COMEDK

Placement

 R V Institute of Technology and Management has released its placement report for 2023. So far, the highest package and lowest package offered during placements 2023 stood at INR 40 LPA and INR 3.5 LPA, respectively.
