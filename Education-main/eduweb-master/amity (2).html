Amity University Noida

Amity University is a private institute that offers higher education through its 9 national and 13 international campuses. Amity University Noida was established in 2005 and is recognized by UGC, AIU, the Department of Science & Technology and the Central Counselling Board. The institute is approved by AICTE and accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade. Amity Noida has been ranked 35th among the top universities in India under NIRF Rankings 2023. 

It offers UG, PG, Industry collaborative, Intergrated, International, Evening, Doctoral and Online programs under streams like Engineering, Management, Law, Design, Medical, etc in full-time, part-time, and distance learning modes. The university campus is spread over an area of 40 acres. It also holds the accrediation of IACBE, BSI, IET UK, ACBSP, BCI, COA, ASIC UK, RCI, PCI, and AERB. Amity Noida is ranked among the top 3% of universities globally by QS and Times Higher Education World University Rankings.

Admission

Amity University Noida offers 300+ programmes in diverse streams ranging from Engineering, Management, Commerce, Design, Law, and Medical to Forensic Sciences, Animation, and Anthropology. The university offers various Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Doctoral courses through regular and distance learning modes in all major streams.

  1. Amity University offers admission to UG and PG courses on both direct and entrance basis.  
  2. Candidates securing 80%+ aggregate in Class XII will be offered Direct Admission to all UG courses. 
  3. Direct Admission is not applicable in Architecture/Design/Education/Fine Arts/Fashion/Law and Physical Education.
  4. Amity University conducts Amity JEE or a Personal Interview for admission to the B.Tech course.
  5. Candidates with a valid score in NATA/ JEE Main Paper II are eligible to apply for admission to the B.Arch course.
  6. mity University Noida also accepts CUET for admission to its UG programs. 
  7. Amity Noida also offers direct admission to its own graduates in all Masters's programs if they meet the eligibility criteria. 
  8. Candidates with a valid score in CAT/MAT/XAT/CMAT/GMAT/NMAT can apply for MBA at Amity University Noida. 
  9. The university conducts a course-based Written Test/ Creative Test/ Physical Test wherever required.
  10. Admission to Ph.D. at Amity University Noida is offered on the basis of  PET (Ph.D. Entrance Test).
  11. Candidates who have cleared UGC NET/CSIR/ICAR (ASRB)/GPAT/ICMR/GATE/ DST-INSPIRE are exempted from PET.

NHCE Admission BE Admission 

Amity University Noida offers admission to 4-year full-time B.Tech. courses under numerous specializations. Amity Noida also offers Integrated B.Tech courses for a 5-years duration. The candidate securing a minimum of 60% marks in aggregate in Class X and XII and 60% in PCM can apply for Amity University Noida B.Tech admission. The applicants are selected based on their merit in the AMITY JEE or JEE Mains examination.

Amity JEE Eligibility

Before applying for Amity JEE, satisfying the eligibility standards is a must. Candidates meeting the criteria can then easily apply for the examination. As per the authorities, the eligibility criteria are defined as per the B.Tech. program, meaning the eligibility varies for each program offered.

General/ Basic Eligibility:

  • Intermediate graduates with Science stream from a recognized board.
  • Presently appearing in 10+2 examination with Science stream can also apply for JEE.
  • A minimum of 60% marks in aggregate in Class 10 and 10+2 examination.
