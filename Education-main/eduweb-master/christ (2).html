Christ University:

Christ University is declared a Deemed to be University with Private ownerships and was established in the year 2008. The university offers a wide range of courses across multiple streams and branches. A total of 136 courses are offered across the diploma, UG, PG and Doctoral levels. Admissions to any of these courses are offered to students who meet the eligibility criteria and follow the prescribed steps of admission for each of them. The university also offers multiple internal and external scholarships that eligible students can avail. A total of 4 colleges are affiliated to Christ University, Bangalore. ​Christ University is ranked 67th among the top 100 universities in India in NIRF Ranking 2023 and accredited by the NAAC with an ‘A+’ Grade

Christ University offers multi-disciplinary UG, PG, and Doctoral programs in the fields of Arts, Science, Commerce, Management, Engineering, Architecture, Law, etc. BTech, MBA, LLM, M.Sc, and M.Tech are flagship courses of Christ University. Admissions to most of the UG and PG programs are done through the Christ University Entrance Test (CUET) followed by Micro Presentation (MP), Personal Interview (PI), and Skill Assessment (SA

Christ University offers great placement opportunities to its students. The placement rate of Christ University is around 70%-80% every year.

Placement:

Christ University Placements

Christ University has a dedicated centre for placement which is known as the Centre for Placements and Career Guidance. Apart from guiding the students through the process of recruitments, the Christ University placement centre is also responsible for career counselling of the students in an attempt to help them make informed careers choices. The christ university placement centre organises campus recruitments every year where it invites reputed companies of the country to conduct interviews
