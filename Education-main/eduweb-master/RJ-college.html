Rajarajeswari Medical College and Hospital (RRMCH)

The Rajarajeswari Medical College and Hospital (RRMCH) was set up by the Moogambigai Charitable and Educational Trust and is situated on Mysore Road Bangalore. The Moogambigai Charitable and Educational Trust serves to develop as a renowned educational institution in the fields of Engineering, Medicine, Dental, Paramedical, Management and other sciences with a special infrastructure for health related issues.

It also provides Lateral Entry for direct admission in the second year for the B.Sc program. Admission to these courses is merit and entrance based. Applicants seeking admission to the institute may apply online as well as offline. The institute is well equipped with all the facilities with the motive of providing premier experience and learning to their students.

RRMCH Admission

The college offers UG, PG, and Ph.D. programs in the field of Medicine. At the UG level, the college offers MBBS and B.Sc. At the PG level, the college offers MD, MS, DM and M.CH. Along with these, the college also offers Ph.D. and Diploma programs. Rajarajeshwari Medical College also provides Lateral Entry for direct admission in the second year for B.Sc program.

  • Applicants who have passed 10+2 with 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English from a recognized board are consideredeligible to apply for MBBS
  • . Admission to MBBS is done on the basis of valid score obtained in NEET UG
  •  Candidates applying for B.Sc. must have passed 10+2 with 50% marks in Science stream from a recognized board to be considered eligible for admission. 
  • For Lateral Entry, candidates must have passed Diploma in Medicine with 50% marks from a recognized university. 
  • Admission to B.Sc. is done on the basis of Merit in 10+2 examination. 
  • Candidates applying for MD and MS must have passed MBBS with 50% marks from a university recognized by MCI. 
  • Admission to MD and MS is done on the basis of valid score obtained in NEET PG. 
  • Candidates applying for DM and M.CH must have passed MD/ MS in the relevant stream with 50% marks from a recognized university.
  •  Admission to DM and M.CH is done on the basis of valid score obtained in NEET SS.
  •  Applicants for Ph.D. must have a Master’s degree in the concerned discipline with 55% marks in order to be eligible for admission.
  • Admission to Ph.D. is done through the marks scored in UGC NET followed by Counselling.
  •  The college offers 5% relaxation in the eligibility criteria for candidates belonging to SC/ST categories.

Hostel

On- Campus accommodation is available to out station students in the 4 spacious hostels. Separate UG and PG hostels for boys and girls accommodate up to 2500 students with a choice of shared and individual rooms. The ladies hostel UG with Ground + 4 floors and UG gents hostel has Ground + 5 floors. Clean and airy, the rooms offer beds, wardrobes and study tables and chairs. NRI quarters with ground + 5 floors and PG quarters with Ground + 5 floors, staff quarters with Ground + 5 floors complete the housing scenario.

A common room with recreational facilities, laundry facilities and mess facilties are features of each hostels, besides a visitors room. Guest faculty, visitors and others are accommodated in well – furnished guest houses which are available on advance intimation and availability. The canteen at RRMCH is yet another facility that adds to the homely atmosphere. The canteen offers appetising fare from snaccks to full fledged, well balanced meals.
