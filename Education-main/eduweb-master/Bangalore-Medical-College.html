Bangalore Medical College and Research Institute (BMCRI) 

Bangalore Medical College and Research Institute (BMCRI) was set up in 1955 by the Mysore Education Society. Originally part of the Mysore University and Bangalore University, the institute later got affiliated to the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences. The institute has got recognition from the Medical Council of India for undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma courses.

The courses offered by BMCRI are MBBS, MD, MS, B.Sc. Nursing, M.Sc. Nursing, DM and M.Ch. Aside from this, it also offers various diploma programs.

 BMCRI Facilities

  • Hostel-The college has hostels for boys and girls. While boys have hostels only for undergraduate students, girls can avail of this facility even at the postgraduate level. There are 300 seats in the boys hostel and 88 in the girls hostel.
  • Research Center-There is a central research center. The research laboratory can be used at nominal costs. Services like Karyotyping of blood and bone marrow are some of things done at this central research centre.
  • Library-There is a two-storey library. Apart from the general library, there is a section with books on SC and ST which can be used only by SC and ST students. Caste certificate needs to be provided to avail this facility.

Hostel

  • The boy’s hostel for undergraduate students accommodates 300 students. Eight rooms have triple sharing facility other six rooms have double sharing facility, and the rest six rooms can accommodate eight students in each room. The undergraduate boy’s hostel also comprises of one office space, one guest room, a dining hall that can accommodate 100 students. There are two study rooms with the accommodation capacity of 60 and 20. Students can use gymnasium present in the hostel. There is a hostel facility for postgraduate students in Shivajinagar Bangalore.
  • The girl’s hostels are also available for undergraduate, postgraduate and internship students. The undergraduate girl’s hostel can accommodate 243 in 81 rooms while there are ten rooms for interns which can accommodate 30 students. There are 20 rooms for postgraduate students, which can accommodate 88 students. The Kaveri ladies hostel is present within the campus, and the hostel for postgraduate female students is in Chamrajpet. Hostel mess provides delicious and hygienic delicacies to the students.
