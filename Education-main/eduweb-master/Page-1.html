New Horizon College of Engineering(NHCE) 

The New Horizon College of Engineering also known as NHCE is a private engineering college situated near Marathahalli, in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. The New Horizon College of Engineering was established in 2001. The NHCE is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), approved by University Grants Commission (UGC) and the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). 

New Horizon College of Engineering offers BE, M.Tech, MBA and MCA programs at UG and PG level respectively. With this New Horizon College of Engineering (NHCE) also offers Ph.D. and M.Sc (Engg.) by Research in different specializations at Doctorate level. Admission to all the courses are based on national and state-level entrance exams.

Admission

  1. Admission to BE program is based on a valid score in KCET conducted by Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA). 
  2. M.Tech, MBA and MCA admissions are based on a valid score in KARNATAKA PGCETollowed by counselling.
  3. GATE qualified candidates are also eligible to apply for M.Tech program.
  4. For MBA program, CAT/ MAT or XAT score are also accepted by NHCE.
  5. While, for MCA, KMAT qualified candidates can also apply.
  6. Ph.D. and M.Sc (Engg.) by Research is granted on the basis of performance in Research Aptitude Test (RAT) followed by an Interview.
  7. Application form can be filled online by visiting the official website followed by submission through post or in person.
