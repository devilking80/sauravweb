Reva University

Reva University is a private university, located in Kattigenahalli, Yelahanka, Bangalore. Established in 2012, the university is accredited by the NAAC with an 'A' Grade. Reva University currently offers 55 Undergraduate Programmes, 28 full-time & 2 Part-time Postgraduate Programmes, 17 Ph.D. Programmes in Engineering, Architecture, Science and Technology, Commerce, Management Studies Law, Arts & Humanities, and Performing Arts. B.Tech is the most popular program of Reva University and admissions to the same are done on the basis of scores in REVA CET/KCET/COMED-K/Uni-GAUGE. B.Tech cutoff at Reva University ranges from 61443-149117 for the KCET exam

Reva University provides excellent placement and internship opportunities to its students. Many top-notch companies from various sectors visit the campus every year to make placement offers. In the placement drive of 2022, a total of 3,512 job offers were made by participating companies. The highest package offered was INR 45 LPA. DELL, DHL, Philips, IBM, Berger, Amazon, Infosys, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Sunrise Systems, and Radcom Technologies are some of the top recruiters at Reva University.

Reva University B.Tech Admission

The university provides Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) and Bachelor of Technology Lateral (B.Tech Lateral) degrees to their students at the graduate level. The course duration of B.Tech is 4 years while B.Tech (Lateral) or the lateral entry needs 3 years to get completed.

Reva University offers five specializations in Bachelor of Technology, that are Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. The specializations are similar to the students who take lateral or second-year entry in the B.Tech.

The students interested in the courses mentioned above must have to go through an entrance test administered by the university itself (REVA CET). The selection will be made according to the score and performance in the entrance examination.

REVA EET 

REVA EET (REVA Universityâ€™s Engineering Entrance Test) is a national-level entrance exam conducted by REVA University for admissions to undergraduate engineering programs - B. Tech. in 7 specializations.

  • The exam is conducted in 16 various exam centers across India.
  • Before filling the application form, all candidates must go through the eligibility criteria. 
  • The application charges for applicants are INR 1000/- (Indian/SAARC Candidates); INR 2000/- (NRI Candidates); US $ 50 (Foreign National Aspirants).
  • The exam will be 2 hours and consist of objective-type questions. 
  • The question paper is divided into three sections namely Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  • The admission to B. Tech. will be based on candidatesâ€™ score and their performance in the interview round.

REVA University Placement

The Placement and Training Cell handles all aspects of campus placements for the graduating students at REVA University. The wing provides employment opportunities to students through campus recruitments, pooled campus, and off-campus recruitments. The recruitment process is initiated with a clear focus on the students from all academic levels. All levels of the companies are contacted and invited for the campus recruitment drive which matches different academic levels of students. The placement department is well equipped with excellent infrastructure to support every stage of the placement process such as arrangements for Pre-Placement Talks, Interviews, Group Discussions, and Workshops.

REVA University Scholarship

The scholarships are awarded to students seeking admissions through merit, sports quota, defense quota, PH quota, or other applicable categories. These scholarships are awarded based on the eligibility criteria decided by the University. The following are the scholarships provided by the university.

  • Student Scoring Rank in Karnataka Common Entrance Test(K-CET): Students who have qualified in K-CET are entitled to receive the scholarship based on their ranks. The percentage of scholarship amount varies from student to student as the scholarship is strictly awarded based on the rank secured in K-CET.
