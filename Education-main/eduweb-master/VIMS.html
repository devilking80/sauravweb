Vydehi Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre{VIMS}

Vydehi Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre (VIMS) was established in the year 2000 and situated in a pleasant, peaceful atmosphere of greenery. It is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS) and approved by Dental Council of India (DCI), Medical Council of India (MCI). It has a comprehensive range of general and super specialty medical services are provided here including complex surgeries to authentic ayurvedic experience at Vydehi AyurvedaGram. The faculty of the institute is highly qualified, experienced, and dedicated in the field of medical, dental science.

The institute focuses on delivering high quality, comprehensive, integrated medical education including innovative thoughts towards modern technology and research. The vision of the institute is to provide a world-class integrated healthcare system, with a focus on compassionate patient care to the underprivileged people. The mission of the institute is to bring healthcare to International standards within the reach of every individual.

 ​VIMS Admissions

Vydehi Institute of Medical Sciences and Research offers MBBS at the undergraduate level based on the score in NEET UG conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). Candidates seeking MBBS admission must score a minimum of 50% Marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology at the 10+2 examination. Candidates with a score in NEET UG are required to register for counseling conducted by KEA.

Hostel

  • Separate hostel with a qualified warden is arranged in the same campus for girls, with full security measures. Hostel is compulsory for all the students admitted to this college. Food is provided in the hostel mess.
  • The entire hostel complexes at Vydehi are ensured with 24-hour security, maintenance, engineering, power, water supply The hostel provides a cot, a mattress, a study table, a chair and a cupboard for each student.
  •  Every hostel has solar heated water facility, water cooler, common telephone, common TV room, common reading room etc.
  • Adequate dining facilities are available for all students.
  • Hostels have gymnasium and indoor games facilities.
  • Mess caters variety of food to suit all tastes. NRI/Foreign student will be provided with single room.
