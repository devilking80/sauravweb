SRMIST , Chennai 

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRM IST), formerly known as SRM University is a Deemed-to-be University located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu was established in 1985. SRM University is approved by AICTE and UGC and is accredited by IET Engineering, the Engineering Accreditation Commission of ABET, the Computing Accreditation Commission of ABET, NBA, and NAAC with A++ grades. SRM Chennai Campus is spread over 250 acres and has a total of six campuses off ering UG, PG and Doctoral programs in the fields of Engineering & Technology, Management, Medicine & Health Sciences, Science & Humanities, Law and Agricultural Sciences. According to NIRF 2023, SRM Chennai is ranked in 18th position under the University category and 32nd position under the Overall category

  • B.Tech and MBA are the flagship courses off ered at SRM University.
  • Admission to all Engineering and Management courses is based on SRMJEEE and SRMJEEM scores.
  • SRM University Chennai off ers admission to MBA courses full-time, part-time as well as on online mode.
  • SRM Chennai has a track record of placing 95% of its eligible students in top companies like TCS, Accenture, Wipro, etc.
  • The university off ers facilities such as sports, a library, a gym, bookstores, laboratories, hostels, and a dining area.

SRM EXAM:

SRM University Entrance Exam

SRM University conducts entrance exams for admission to its various courses. The admission tests are conducted separately for different branches of study

SRMJEEE

SRMJEEE Or SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Exam is conducted for admission to B.Tech. programs off ered at SRM University campuses across Chennai, Haryana, and Andhra Pradesh. The minimum eligibility requirement for applying to SRMJEEE is 10+2 in the science streamwith a minimum of 60% marks.

SRMJEEE 2023 Exam Pattern 

SRMJEEE has five sections such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology, English and Aptitude. Candidates opting for B.Tech Biotechnology, Biomedical Engineering, 

Bioinformatics and Genetic Engineering have to attempt the Biology section instead of Mathematics. 

  • The duration of SRMJEEE 2023 is 2 hours 30 minutes. 
  • The exam paper consists of 125 multiple-choice questions and 1 mark will be awarded for
    each question. 
  • There is no negative marking. 
  • The application fee for SRM JEEE 2023 is INR 1200

SRMJEEM  

SRM JEEM is the qualifying joint entrance exam for MBA programmes at SRM University Campuses. Candidates with a Bachelor’s degree with a minimum aggregate of 60% are eligible for the examination. There is no age limit. SRM JEEM will be conducted only at 8 test centres all over India. The application fee of 1100 INR can be paid online or off line through a DD in the name of SRM University.

SRMJEEM 2023 Exam Pattern 

SRM JEEM 2023 is a computer-based test conducted by SRM University Chennai. The duration of the exam is 2 hours and 30 minutes. The exam paper consists of 5 sections namely: Data Interpretation, Quantitative, Reasoning, English, GK/CA/BA. Each section consists of 20 questions and each question carries 3 marks. The candidates must answer 100 objective-type questions. There is no negative marking

Results: 

SRM Institute of Science & Technology (SRM IST) formerly known as SRM University Chennai publishes the results of the Term-End examinations and results for the entrance examination (SRMJEEE) on the official website. Students can log in using their credentials to check their results on the university’s website. SRM Institute of Science & Technology offers multiferous courses at graduate, postgraduate and doctoral levels. Only students with already created Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) accounts can view their results on the portal. 

SRM University Semester Result 2023

Candidates can view their semester results & scorecard by following the given procedure:

  • Visit the Official website of SRM University & select the concerned university campus 
  • Click on the exam section followed by the result.
  • On the following page enter your Registration number, Date of Birth (DOB) and verification
    code. 
  • Click on the “submit” button to view the result 
  • The corresponding result status will be displayed on the screen. 
  • Save and take the printout of the scorecard for future reference 

Students looking forward to their results must keep in mind that the result displayed on the website is only to provide immediate information to the students. In order to receive accurate information about the result, students are advised to contact the respective department of SRM University.

SRM University Facilities

SRM University Chennai is a private university spread across 250 acres. SRM Chennai has state-of-the-art infrastructure and other facilities to accommodate students on campus. Some of the facilities off ered by the university are as follows: 

  • Central Library : SRM Library is nearly three decades old. The library consists of books,
    journals, e-journals and other resources in various branches and is equipped with reading
    halls on all the floors. The central library is automated with RFID (Radio Frequency
    Identification) technology, Centralized Air Conditioned (AC), and well protected with
    CCTV Security systems and fire alarms. 
  • Hostels: SRM Chennai provides comfortable housing facilities for all students. The
    university has separate hostels for girls, boys and international students. SRM hostels
    off er a wide range of dining options such as North Indian, South Indian, Thai, Chinese and
    Continental food to satisfy the needs of students from across the world.
  • Canteens: SRMIST Canteens are located on diff erent campuses in Chennai and off er a
    variety of foods, snacks and beverages at a minimal cost. The canteens are open from
    7:00 AM to 5:00 PM on the old campus and at the University building. 
  • Counselling Services: The university also off ers free counselling dedicated to students,
    to cater to the well-being of students.       
  • Sports: SRM University off ers indoor and outdoor facilities for games such as Volleyball,
    Football, Tennis, Badminton, Hockey and Cricket Fields. The university also houses a gym,yoga, swimming pools, indoor stadiums and A/C multipurpose indoor stadiums to facilitate the overall well-being of students. 
  • Other Facilities and Services: The university off ers other services such as Maintenace,
    House Keeping Services, Security & Fire Safety, Minor Construction, Waste Treatment,
    Transportation, ATMs, Bookstores, CCTV Security Systems and Prayer Halls. 
  • Clubs: SRM University Chennai focuses on the holistic development of students and
    encourages them to participate in co-curricular activities. The university has various
    clubs for students to participate in such as Rotaract Club, NSS, NCC, Astrophilia, Fashion,
    Music, Literature Club, Festival, Dance Club, Movies and Dramatics, Sports, Creative Arts
    & Media, Women Empowerment, Self Defense Club, Social Club, Gaming and Quiz. 
  • Arts & Culture: The university celebrates and organizes various festivals and cultural
    events throughout the year. Students are encouraged to participate in events and fests
    for recreational purposes. Some of the events celebrated are Milan (The cultural fest),
    Shuru (The Icebreaker), Jhalak, Yoga Day, Gokulashtami, Matki Breaking and many more.
