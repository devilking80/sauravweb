Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology(SMVIT)

Sir M Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology is a reputed institute in Karnataka established by Sri Krishnadevaraya Educational Trust in 1986. The Institute is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University and approved by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi and is accredited by National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi. 

 The institute offers a total of 20 courses, including BE, MTech, MCA, MBA, Integrated PhD, PhD, and MS By Research. These courses are offered in affiliation with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU). The 133-acre campus of the institute has 2,800 students and nearly 250 faculty members. SMVIT B.Tech Admission

Sir MVIT offers B.E. programs in 8 specializations namely; Civil, Mechanical, Electrical & Electronics, Electronics & Communication, Computer Science & Engineering, Industrial Engineering & Management, Telecommunication, Information Science and Bio Technology and Masters Program with 4 specializations like; Computer Applications, Business Administration, Computer Integrated Manufacturing and Electronics. 

  • Candidates applying for B.E should have passed 10+2 with 45%.
  • Candidates must qualify either K-CET/COMED-K/JEE Mains. 
  • Students belonging to Karnataka can appear for K-CET only, while other Indian students can appear for either of the 3 entrances.
  • M.Tech admissions are offered to those students who have a bachelor’s degree in Engineering & Technology in a relevant discipline. 
  • Candidates applying for M.Tech must qualify either GATE/PGCET entrance to be eligible for admissions.
  • The students must have passed bachelor’s degree in any discipline for MBA and MCA. 
  • For MBA and MCA, selection of candidates are made on the basis of the KMAT/PGCET Score. Candidates applying for MCA must have Mathematics at 10+2 or graduation level for MCA.
  • Candidates have to fill the Alliance University Bangalore application form for admission to the B.Tech course.
  • Then, candidates have to appear and qualify any one of the following examinations: JEE (Main)/JEE (Advanced)/Karnataka CET/COMEDK/Alliance University Scholastic Aptitude Test (AUSAT); or any other State-level engineering entrance examination.
  • Further, candidates who qualified the entrance exam have to appear in a personal interview.
  • The final selection of the candidate will be based on the overall performance in the Alliance admission selection process.
  • Final admission is confirmed only after verification of documents and course fee payment.

SMVIT Placement

The training and placement cell of Sir MVIT is engaged in developing the students in all skills needed by recruiting companies and placing them in top companies. Students are also advised to attain seminars and workshops etc. Right from the fourth semester onwards, regular training is conducted on total personality development and personal grooming of the students. A minimum of 35 to 40 top companies visit the college every year and 90 – 100 per cent of students are placed. 4811 no. of offers were made in the last five years from which 3301 students got placed. Three students got placed in Lam Research Company from the Mechanical branch with more than 11 LPA CTC.
